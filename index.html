Ryo is known for her cool, solitary demeanor and androgynous appearance. She often exudes a mysterious aura, characterized by her expressionless face and quiet nature. Despite her aloof exterior, Ryo has a playful side and enjoys being called a "weirdo." She has a penchant for eccentric hobbies and a dry sense of humor, often engaging in unconventional activities like eating weeds when she's hungry and spending all her money on musical instruments.
